Beer
Pong (Duos)

The classic beer game to get the night started right!

Here are the rules for Beer Pong:

1) The Cup Formation is 3-2-1 (6 cups).

2) During offense, the players can choose between a standard shot and a bounce shot.

3) During offense, the players must keep their elbows behind the table, or the shot will not be counted.

4) Both players from one team are given a ping pong ball to shoot and may do so simultaneously.

5) Both players from the current team must have taken a shot before the other team can shoot.

6) A standard shot is worth 1 cup.

7) A bounce shot is worth 2 cups.

8) If both shooters score with a normal shot in the same cup, the opposing team loses 3 cups.

9) If the shooters score one bounce and one normal shot, the opposing team loses 3 cups. If they score in the same cup, the opposing team loses 5 cups.

10) If both shooters score a bounce shot the opposing team loses 4 cups. If they score in the same cup, the opposing team loses 6 cups.

11) A bounce shot can be blocked.

12) Illegal blocks cost the defenders one cup.

13) If the shooters win with one cup left, the opposing team is given 2 shots to try to extend the game into overtime.

Blitz
Krieg (Solo)

Just like the good ole' Beer Pong, except you are solo, fighting one on one!

Here are the rules for Blitz Krieg. It is a game just like Beer Pong, except there is only one player per team:

1) The Cup Formation is 2-1 (3 cups).

2) During offense, the player can choose between a standard shot and a bounce shot.

3) During offense, the player must keep his elbows behind the table, or the shot will not be counted.

4) The standard shot is worth 1 cup

5) The bounce shot is worth 2 cups

6) A bounce shot can be blocked.

7) An Illegal block costs the defender one cup.

8) If the shooter wins with one cup left, the opponent is given a chance to extend the game into overtime.
