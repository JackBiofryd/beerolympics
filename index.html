What is Beer
Olympics?

Hello everyone! This is the official page of Beer Olympics. This year you will have the opportunity to attend the second annual Beer Olympics. Our main goal is to get people together, listen to good music, party hard and of course, drink beer and have a good time!


The first annual Beer Olympics was an extreme success where people had more fun than ever. We hope we can do it again this year by making this a special night for everyone. Win many awards as well as someone's heart. Have a great time! May the best drinkers win!
