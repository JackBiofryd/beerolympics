Beer
Pong

The classic beer game to get the night started right!

Any Hole is Our Goal!

Here are the rules for Beer Pong:

1. The Cup Formation is 3-2-1 (6 cups).

2. During offense, the player can choose between the standard shot and the bounce shot.

3. Both players from one team are given a ping pong ball to shoot and may do so simultaneously.

4. Both players from the current team must have taken a shot before the other team can shoot.

5. The standard shot is worth 1 cup

6. The bounce shot is worth 2 cups

7. If both members hit the same cup it's worth 3 cups.

8. If both members hit the same cup with a bounce shot it's worth 4 cups.

9. The bounce shot can be blocked.

10. Illegal blocks give the opposite team another shot.
